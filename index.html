About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineer, Inventor, Coder, Volunteer, Father, and Granddad. I've worked as a Mechanical Engineer for the same company for 30 years, creating electric actuators, motors, and other electromagnetic devices.

On the side I also run my own, small, individual inventor business, where I've created devices for the home and written software, including a Chrome Extension. My next step is to expand this passion for coding into a new career.

Finally, I've spent many years volunteering to bring a Waldorf inspired charter school to life in Fort Collins for my children and grandchildren.

Creating, learning, and being with my family bring me the most joy in life.
